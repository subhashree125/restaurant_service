Featured Dishes

Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh is a fragrant and flavorful dish, featuring tender lamb pieces slow-cooked in a rich, aromatic blend of yogurt, browned onions, and spices like cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. This Kashmiri classic is known for its deep red color derived from mild Kashmiri chili and its sumptuous, velvety texture.

Malai Kofta

Malai Kofta is a rich and creamy North Indian delicacy, featuring soft, melt-in-your-mouth paneer and vegetable dumplings in a luscious tomato and cashew nut gravy. It's a perfect blend of spices and creaminess, offering a luxurious vegetarian experience.

Dahi Bhalla

Dahi Bhalla, a popular North Indian delicacy, features soft lentil fritters soaked in creamy yogurt, garnished with tangy tamarind chutney and aromatic spices. This refreshing and flavorful dish offers a perfect blend of sweet, sour, and savory tastes, making it an irresistible treat.
